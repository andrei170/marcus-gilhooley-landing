FAQ

Common Questions

How does the Click Club placement work?

Top students complete the 4-week training and interview for our own agency sales team. If we have openings and they're a fit, they join us. This is paid market-rate commissions (8-12% of deal value). We pay referral fees internally so the economics make sense.

What if I'm not placed with Click Club?

Tier 2 (partner network) and Tier 3 (open market with support) are your paths. Most students who put in the reps land a Tier 2 role within 60-90 days.

Is this UK-only?

No. Global. UK students get extra attention because the market's my origin. We place across US, UK, EU, and Middle East regularly.

Do I need sales experience?

No. Most of my students have zero sales experience. The training is designed for beginners — tonality, scripts, objection handling, pipeline discipline all taught from scratch.

Who is this NOT for?

Anyone wanting a passive income shortcut. Closing is active work. The income is great; the work is real.
